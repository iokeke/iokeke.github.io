1.Arizona

  • CREATIVITY is the first thing that comes to mind everytime I see the arizona state website which is why this is my favorite state. Instead of being like every other state's website by putting all the main info in tabs at the top of the website. Arizona put the most important aspects of their state in boxes which you can click on the arrows to go from one to the next. The website is very visual instead of being filled with a bunch of words all over the home page.
  • The background image has an orange tab that gives a brief explanation of whatever the image might be.(e.g the background is the boulder dam if the tab is clicked on a brief explanation of the boulder dam pops up.
  • Pictures are attached to almost every tab or link on the main page.
  • No problems having this website on my phone or tablet or any devices it is a responsive website.
